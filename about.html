About Pixel Phantoms

"From Students, For Students."

Our Mission

Pixel Phantoms is a student-driven tech club dedicated to empowering learners through structured learning modules, mentorship, project reviews, doubt-clearing sessions, submissions, leaderboards, hackathons, and technical activities.

Focus Areas

Web Development

Full-stack development with modern frameworks and tools

Programming

Data structures, algorithms, and competitive coding

Design

UI/UX design principles and creative visual solutions

Embedded Systems

IoT, microcontrollers, and hardware programming

Open Source

Collaborative development and community contributions

Community Learning

Peer-to-peer learning and knowledge sharing
